Afterward, he prostrated himself before the gods <a href="https://www.britannica.com/topic/Anu" class="md-crosslink " data-show-preview="true">An</a> (Anu) and <a href="https://www.britannica.com/topic/Enlil" class="md-crosslink " data-show-preview="true">Enlil</a>, and, as a reward for living a godly life, Ziusudra was given immortality.</p><!--[MOD2]--><span class="marker MOD2 mod-inline"></span></section>
