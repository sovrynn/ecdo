The story indicates that Szeukha, who was saved by floating on a ball of pine resin, landed on a mountain above the Salt River, and his cave and tools can still be seen there. Additionally, the Creator advised people to seek refuge on Crooked Mountain, where some survived as well. Therefore, it can be concluded that the following lands/mountains did not get flooded:

1. The mountain above the Salt River
2. Crooked Mountain

Additionally, it is mentioned that Szeukha survived in a cave.
