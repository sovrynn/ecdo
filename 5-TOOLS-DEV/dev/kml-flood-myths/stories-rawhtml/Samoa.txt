<p>In a battle between Fire and Water (offspring of the
primeval octopus), everything was overwhelmed by a
'boundless sea', and the god Tangaloa had the task of
re-creating the world. [<a href="#Poignant">Poignant</a>,
p. 30]</p>
<p>The only survivor of a deluge was a man or a lizard
named Pili, who, by marriage with the stormy petrel, begat
offspring to repopulate the land. [<a href="#Frazer">Frazer</a>, p. 249]</p>
