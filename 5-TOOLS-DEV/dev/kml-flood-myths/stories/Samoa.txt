In a battle between Fire and Water (offspring of the
primeval octopus), everything was overwhelmed by a
'boundless sea', and the god Tangaloa had the task of
re-creating the world. [Poignant,
p. 30]
The only survivor of a deluge was a man or a lizard
named Pili, who, by marriage with the stormy petrel, begat
offspring to repopulate the land. [Frazer, p. 249]
